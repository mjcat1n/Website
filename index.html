About Me

Hi—I’m Matt, a soon-to-be Computer Science graduate from Metropolitan State University of Denver. I am currently working on building my portfolio in hopes to stand out in this competitive job market. I have worked on various projects inconsistently over the past decade and a half, but have not been consistent in my coding journey until recently. Some of my projects include a Twitter image bot that posts photos from Reddit made in Python, a Telegram gambling chatbot made in Python, a Jeopardy browser game made in C++, and many others. I have dabbled in web development, but this is my first attempt at creating and maintaining a portfolio. I look forward to building up my skills and showcasing my work here and on my github. If you are interested in my work, or would like to hire me, please feel free to reach out using the contact form below. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
